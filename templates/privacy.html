6. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • User database backups — rolling 14-day backups kept on the same server volume
  • Stock price cache — automatically refreshed every 12 hours; not personal data
  • Server logs — retained for up to 30 days by Fly.io infrastructure
  • Stripe billing records — retained as required by tax and financial regulations (typically 7 years)

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — update your email, nickname or preferences via your profile
  • Erasure — delete your account instantly via Settings → Delete Account; this removes your email, password hash, usage data, and watchlist from our systems
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time

To exercise any right, email info@gultechs.net. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. In the UK: ICO (ico.org.uk). In the EU: your national data protection authority.

8. Data Security

  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS enforced)
  • The application server and volume are hosted in Fly.io's Amsterdam (AMS) region within the EU
  • Stripe handles all card data — we never see or store payment card numbers

9. Children

Stocker is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us and we will delete the account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when we add new features or if regulations change. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered users. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
